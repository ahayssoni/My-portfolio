(01) — About

A physician who reads papers, prompts, and patients with the same patience.

01 · Bedside

Trained in medicine. Curious about machines. My day-to-day is the bedside — neurological exams, ward rounds, the small detective work of finding what a brain is trying to say. My after-hours work is asking what happens when the next stethoscope is a model.

02 · Framework

I focus on AI governance in healthcare: the policy, the audit trail, the consent — the unsexy scaffolding that decides whether a tool helps a patient or harms them. And on utilization: putting these systems into the hands of clinicians without breaking the trust that took a century to build.
